Thieves of Capitol

Last updated: February 7, 2026

An amount of years before the war with the Elves, during the reign of Emperor Leto, the Undying, a group of thieves tries to make a name for themselves in the bustling streets of Capitol. In this second chapter, the player's fledgling crew, "The Baker's Dozen," expands and establishes themselves as independent actors. > After the loss of their patron, Joey Silver-Thumbs, and the flight of their friend Shel, "The Bakers Dozen," rebuilds their crew and forges a network of their own.

Additional Information

The Veiled

This demon worshipping cult of gangsters was utterly decimated by the party's actions in chapter one. If any members remain they're keeping their survival quite secret.

House Rosencrans

This family is currently living in exile as a result of the party's actions in chapter one.

House Tantalas

House Tantalas is slowly recovering from the scandal of chapter one. Their young patriarch, Alexandar, struggles to maintain the family's legacy.

House Valencia

Raymon Valencia has been building his trade network in a far more reserved manner since the loss of his eldest daughter, Katherine.
