    Season of Skullduggery

    Session 2: Thefri's Hunting Trophy

    March 1, 2026

    The “Baker's Dozen” has been hired to break into House Kleidemos to recover a hunting trophy for their client, Thefri Hercle. Thefri is anxious for its return and may go ahead without you, costing a valuable payday.

    Gordon's Job

    All any dwarf drinking at Hammer Hall has been able to talk about lately is the annual Gnomish tribute from Whistleport. Three trade vessels packed with precious and semi-precious gems and stones.

    This delivery is one of the most secure secrets in Capitol. No one knows when it comes or on what ships but if the rumors can be believed the treasure will arrive in two days time on the Bellmont, Warren and Regent.

    The tribute will arrive under guard of the feared 4th Legion and its said it will be greeted by the Imperial Guard but the road from port to treasury is long.

    If successful, each member of the "Baker's Dozen" will be able to carry away 2,500 g.p.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Underworld. The first ever to pull off this heist will be the envy of the underworld.

    - Rep: The Crown, Nobility, Faith and Authorities. Tribute pays for public works, temples and defrays taxes.
