    Season of Skullduggery

    Session 5: Ovys the Oracular

    March 30, 2026

    The “Baker's Dozen” delved into the ruins of a dwarven city far beneath the streets of Capitol, to make an offering to a statue of the Dwarven God of Ale, Trim.

    Three days pass. With no public progress toward finding the criminals responsible for stealing from the Gnomish tribute the Emperor's patrols begin to wane.

    Ryan Wythyneye's Job

    The Rosencrans absence from Capitol, together with dwindling traffic to their warehouses have the leaders of Dock Street looking to expand. But to make it work they'll need legitimacy and that means deeds.

    If someone could provide the original they'd be handsomely rewarded.

    They offer a 160 g.p. if the deed to the main warehouse can be found. A bonus of 50 g.p. would be paid for deeds to the other two warehouses. But no one can know they've sponsored the crime and it needs to be done by dawn.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Dock Street, the Underworld

    - Rep: Capitol's Nobility

    Tiny Tim's Job

    The Guard have long suspected the Sorceress Miksa Nikoletta of being involved in the illegal drug trade. They have suspicions but no evidence and moving against her without it could be disastrous.

    They offer a 200 g.p. reward to the crew who can break in and find the evidence they need. "Leave no trace," they say, "and make sure no one knows you were there. If Miksa Nikoletta finds out, there will be consequences for all of us."

    The guard needs the job done in the next 48 hours, after that time the Gray Cloaks will be released from their patrol duties.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: The Guard, The Faith

    - Rep: The Imperial College of Arcane Mysteries
