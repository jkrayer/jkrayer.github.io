    Session 1: The Kidnapping of Lysandra

    February 22, 2026

    Lysandra, the 15-year-old daughter of Arch Cleric Astora has been kidnapped by river pirates. They've demanded a 5,000 g.p. ransom but Astora can only scrape together 500 g.p. She's asked you to solve the problem offering you the 500 g.p. as a reward.

    The exchange is set to take place at the Belaying Pin tomorrow at 8:00 PM, giving you just 26 hours to find the girl before the ransom it due.
