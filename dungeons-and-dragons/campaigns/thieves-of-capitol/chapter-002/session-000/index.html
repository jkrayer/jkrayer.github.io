    Season of Skullduggery

    Session 0

    February 15, 2026

    Autumn winds whip frosty mountain air through the streets of Capitol foretelling the coming of winter. Pastoral places ply their vocation in earnest as the harvest season comes to a close. Livestock and grain are driven into the city from the surrounding countryside. Trade vessels have begun returning ahead of the winter ice flows.

    Shop windows swell with colorful costumes, sundries and frivolities as the city prepares for Panaxius; the city-wide masquerade festival that marks the end of harvest and trade for the year.

    Folks gather their cloaks closer as they bustle through the streets preparing for the festival. The crown commissions bakers and butchers to prepare delights for the citizens while the temples and theaters decorate for their rituals and entertainments.

    But the underworld prepares differently. For heavy clothes conceal forbidden weapons and armor and for a single day crime can be completely anonymous.

    So begins the Season of Skullduggery.

    B.F. Bagman's Job

    Several days ago B.F. was wandering the city and noticed an older fellow clumsily casing House Kleidemos. After casually observing this human elder over the course of several days he decided to approach him and offer the crew's services.

    It seems Gianni Kleidemos once stole a hunting trophy from Thefri (the client) and he'd very much like it back. The trophy is a white claw from an especially large lion. The Kleidemos family keeps exotic war and hunting trophies so Thefri believes it's displayed among them.

    He's offered 200gp for its return but he's growing anxious to have it back. This task must be completed within 72 hours.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Thefri (he seems unaligned)

    - Rep: House Kleidemos, the nobility

    Dell's Job

    The gossip in the guilds says a personal item of Arch-Cleric Philomelos of Hyperion went missing after the porters delivered a marble slab. Word is the Dock Street Boys are hunting the thief but a clever crew could beat them to the punch.

    It's a faith so the coin may be sparse but a favor owed by a major faith has a value of its own.

    Dock Street has a head start so we have at best 48 hours.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Philomelos, The Temple of Hyperion and the Faith.

    - Rep: Dock Street Boys

    Uma's Job

    High Priestess Astora of Axius has been buying her daily bread at precisely 7 AM every day for several months. This morning she was a no show. Uma grew concerned and decided to bring bread to Astora's home.

    There Uma learned that Astora's eldest daughter, Lysandra, was kidnapped by River Pirates and is being held for ransom. Astora's family can only scrape together 500 g.p. a mere 10% of the 5000 g.p. ransom. Astora is understandably beside herself. She'll award the party the 500 g.p. if they can return her daughter safely.

    The exchange takes place tomorrow night at the Belaying Pin.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Astora, The Temple of Axius and the Faith

    - Rep: River Pirates
