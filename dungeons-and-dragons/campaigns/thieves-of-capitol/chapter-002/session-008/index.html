    Kobblestone's Job (Una)

    Though the night's work was long and business has slowed to a crawl, Una keeps the bakery open. Somewhat after 10:00 AM as her head lolls in exhaustion, the bell rings snapping her to semi-consciousness. Three diminutive figures, the tops of their heads barely visible over the counter, saunter in.

    As she comes to the low counter to greet her patrons the unmistakable earthen smell of mines momentarily contorts her face in surprise. ”Halflings hate the mines,“ she begins to think but before she can utter a greeting the lead figure draws back his hood and speaks.

    “Greetings, Miss Una Undervoot, I am Angus Kobblestone, free Gnome. Your people and mine have suffered at the hands of this Empire. Yours in betrayal and war, mine in slavery.“

    Before Angus can say more Una hustles around the counter barring the door and closing the shades. The Gnomish people are indentured to the Empire for 1,000 years, the City is not a safe place for Angus and his friends.

    “I see you know the danger my people face. How much more will it multiply if we can not pay our tribute? My people's suffering brings me to you now. Free Gnomes must strike a blow against tyranny!“ Angus's voice becomes louder with excitement. Remembering his inside voice he continues, “We have a job for you. We need you to get into the Imperial Palace and map their vault and its defenses for us. You must be as accurate as possible and you must do this without leaving a trace.“

    Angus offers a reward of 230 g.p.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Kobblestone's Killers

    - Rep: None if done without a trace
