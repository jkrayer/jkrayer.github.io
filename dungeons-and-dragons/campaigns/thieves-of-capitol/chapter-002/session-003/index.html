    Session 3: Gnomish Tribute

    March 15, 2026

    The “Baker's Dozen” has learned of the Gnomish tribute to Empire's arrival. It's one of the most heavily guarded treasures to arrive in Capitol but also a literal king's ransom.

    Tiny Tim's Job

    There's a rumor going around that Anton Rosencrans is sneaking back into the City tomorrow night and someone's put a price on his head.

    A 1,000 g.p. award is being offered by an unknown benefactor if he can be captured.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Underworld, Nobility, Faith, Authorities, The Benefactor.

    - Rep: The Vieled.

    Ryan Wythyneye's Job

    Left-eyed Loukas, a stern looking fellow with the bearing of a soldier, is looking for a crew to get him into Arch-Wizard Kostas Kleidemos home. He wishes to steal an item from the home but won't identify it. He's insistent that the job must be done without leaving any trace of the theft.

    "Loukas is shopping around for a crew so being first is important. If we can get the job done in the next 48 hours we can beat the other crews to the job."

    A 230 g.p. for each member of the crew.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Unknown Benefactor.

    - Rep: The Imperial College of Arcane Mysteries, Kostas Kleidemos.
