    Season of Skullduggery

    Session 4: Return of Rosencrans

    March 22, 2026

    The “Baker's Dozen” has learned of that Anton Rosencrans is planning to sneak back into the City. They seek the time and location of his arrival.

    The Party's Job

    Sewer Rats have been disappearing in unusual places. Not near the lair of the garbage monster or in the various tunnels choked by giant spider webs (both of which are well known and avoided) but in the more mundane tunnels that run under the city. No bodies have been found, their numbers simply dwindle while friends disappear leaving their worldly possessions behind.

    It's not much compared to your recent exploits, but Philthy hopes you'll come to their aid.

    The Rats offer a 100 g.p. reward for each member of the crew. They need it done before any more go missing.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: The Sewer Rats

    - Rep: unknown

    Ovys the Oracular

    Ovys the Oracular, sometime advisor to House Tantalas, has surreptitiously sought you out after a rousing recommendation from Alexandar, or so his letter goes. He needs a task completed with the utmost discretion. The details to be disclosed in person at his abode.

    “Competition confounds, immediate attention desperately desired.”

    He does disclose an award, 100 g.p. for each member of the crew and a magical item.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Ovys the Oracular, The Imperial College of Arcane Mysteries.

    - Rep: unknown
