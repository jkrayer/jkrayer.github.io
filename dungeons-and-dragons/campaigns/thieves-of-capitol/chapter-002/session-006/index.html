    Season of Skullduggery

    Session 6: The Trial

    April 12, 2026

    The “Baker's Dozen” is put on trial for the crime of Arson. They're found “responsible” and fined 2,500 g.p. Their reputation is severely damaged by the association with the crime.

    In the proceeding days Dell is relieved of his membership in the Carpenters Guild and business at the bakery slows to a halt. Neighbors who once stopped for a friendly chat now pass by quickly and avoid eye contact. Even as martial law is slowly rescinded the presence of guards in Spelt Square and indeed anywhere you go remains suspiciously high.

    As the sun sets on the seventh days since your release a red-scarfed youth enters Spelt Square and dismisses the guards with a deft exchange of coins. Skinny Pete and two others enter the bakery with a proposition.

    Skinny Pete's Job

    Yor blatant disregard for the law has caught the attention of some powerful people. Most think you're reckless, but I think you're just the right kind of dangerous. I have a job for you, if you're interested, if you can complete it, I'll guarantee you a top spot as specialists.

    Archie O'Tierney, current Porters Guild Master is demanding too much, sacrificing too little. He's stonewalling. Trying to get to me. You're going to get to him first.

    Get it done in the next 48 hours, the more bloody and public the better. There's 200 g.p. in it for each if you … I'll kick in a bonus 100 g.p. each if you can get to him in the guild hall during the day.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Fledgling Thieve's Guild

    - Rep: Porter's Guild

    A Mysterious Note

    After Skinny Pete leaves, you find a note attached to the front door of the bakery. Pete's crew was watching the entrance and all of you the front, yet no one saw the note being placed.

    “An associate of mine met an unfortunate end. His body resides in the West Dock Side guard station. Bring the head to the old Graveyard in 24 hours and I'll reward you in gold.”

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: Unknown Benefactor

    - Rep: ?

    Rebel Alchemists

    Later that evening, as you discuss the note and Skinny Pete's proposition, your chat is interrupted by a sudden fierce pounding at the Bakery door. Outside a small woman, her face obscured by a hood, her chest heaving with each breath, glances around nervously and quietly pleads for entrance.

    After entering the petite woman stays close to the door as if ready to flee. She pulls back her hood revealing a nervous care-worn face and brown hair shot through with gray. “I‘m sorry for bringing this to your door but I had nowhere else to go. Friends and I broke into the tower of the Wizard Vibaris to steal back a book stolen from us. We failed and Vibaris hunts me even now. Please go to Vibaris tower and retrieve the book, “Devilish Distillations” by Oriana Andal. Please, find the man with the green scarf at the Lamplighter. Tell him Pisto sent you and give him this.”

    Pisto hands you a small golden pin, a gothic letter A just like the label on the potion of Invisibility. Then disappears into the night in panicked flight from Vibaris.

    Pisto doesn't offer you a reward but doing a favor for the Rebel Alchemists may prove beneficial. If you're going to go you have to go now.

    Reputation Yield

    + Rep: The Rebel Alchemists

    - Rep: None if you succeed
