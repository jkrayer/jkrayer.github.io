    Claw of the Grimalkin

    March 1, 2026

    A Claw of the Grimalkin is created when one such creature is slain by malice and its claws are harvested for trophies.

    The wielder of this talisman receives -20% to their Move Silently and Hide in Shadows rolls. If the wielder doesn't have these skills the claw's magic grants them at a 20% chance of success. Additionally the claw grants the wielder +1/+1 to melee attacks.

    Cursed. When the claw is first touched and at dusk everyday thereafter, the wielder of the claw must make a saving throw vs. Spells. This save is made at -4 if the wielder is in an enchanted forest. If the save fails the wielder becomes a Grimalkin under the DM's control until the following dawn. Once the user fails a number of saves equal to their Charisma score they become a Grimalkin permanently.

    For every 2 failed saving throws the wielder receives an additional +1/+1 to melee attacks.

    A remove curse spell breaks the bond between wielder and claw forever leaving the former wielder both longing for the claw's power and horrified by the loss of self under its influence.

    While the wielder is under the influence of the claw's curse they are thrilled by its power and highly secretive about the source. If any attempt is made to separate the wielder from the claw the wielder defends the talisman fiercely. The wielder will make all attempts to recover it if lost, evade capture or pursuit and slay friends if left no other option.

    When the claw is first touched the wielder will receive a vision of a primeval forest at night. If the wielder fails the first save their vision is violent and they become immediately aware of its powers.
