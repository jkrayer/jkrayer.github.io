Proposal: Thieves of Capitol

September 7, 2025

500 years before the war with the Elves during the reign of Emperor Leto, the Undying a group of thieves tries to make a name for themselves in the bustling streets of Capitol1. In this first chapter the player's fledgling crew will become embroiled in a murder plot that they must resolve to survive.

Abstract

Start Date: T.B.D.

Structure: At the end of each adventure the players will be presented with two jobs2 and must select one. The DM will prepare that job for the following session. All jobs2 will be offered by the patron of the crew, Joey Silver-Thumbs.

Sessions: 5 - 7. I will do my best to keep each session to a single evening's play.

Session 0: Characters will be created, questions answered and the first two jobs2 presented.

Pregenerated Characters: See Below

Experience: Special rules for awarding experience to emphasize mission and objective completion over combat.

Special Equipment: Sap. A successful back-stab attack made with a sap renders the target unconscious. The target must have hit dice less than or equal to the wielders back-stab multiplier, be a medium sized or smaller humanoid and must not be wearing metal armor. The target is rendered unconscious for d4 + back-stab multiplier rounds, d4 + 2 for a first level Thief. A natural 20 doubles the duration.

Reputation: Reputation with other factions starts at 0 (unknown) and may be adjusted up or down by 1-2 points after each mission.

Conclusion: At the end of this Chapter a second chapter or new campaign may be proposed.

Play test: The Assassin, Bard and adjustments to the Fighter and Thief abilities will be tested for balance and fun.
