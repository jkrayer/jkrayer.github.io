Batch #7: Neshanic Valley Alfalfa Blossom

Part of Experimentation Matrix 2

A hydromel strength semi-sweet mead.

Assumptions

Based on Batch #6 I'll make this mead by adding the 5g packet of yeast directly to the must with no re-hydration or nutrients and see if that affects the final flavor. I may re-attempt cold crashing with this batch. #3 is yeasty but also very clear.

Ingredients

  • Poland Spring Water (108 oz planned, 112 oz actual)
  • 2 pounds Neshanic Valley Alfalfa Blossom Honey
    • 1.5 pounds for primary fermentation (actual: 1 lb. 8.2 oz)
    • 0.5 pounds for back-sweetening
  • 5 grams Lalvin ICV D47 Yeast
