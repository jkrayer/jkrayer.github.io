Batch #12: A Cyser using Neshanic Valley Dutch Clover Honey + Zeigler's Honey Crisp Apple Cider

Part of Experimentation Matrix 3

After making several traditional meads, I wanted to try a cyser (mead made with apple juice). Since it's the fall I want to try with apple ciders as well.

Assumptions

It seems the average gravity for a cyser is around 1.100-1.200. Not sure yet what the gravity of the Zeigler's Honey Crisp Apple Cider is, so I'll measure it before adding honey. The recipes I glanced over used around 2-3 pounds of honey per gallon so I'll determine the amount of honey to use after measuring the gravity of the apple juice.

I expect this batch to go to 1.000 SG and then to back-sweeten with honey to reach the final gravity. It's October so temperature may be a factor.

Ingredients

  • 108 oz Zeigler's Honey Crisp Apple Cider
