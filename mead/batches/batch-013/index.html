Batch #13: Neshanic Valley Blueberry Blossom Hydromel

Part of Experimentation Matrix 2

Batch #7 was well received so I'm looking to refine my process to reproduce the result.

Assumptions

I think what went wrong in Batch #6 was using too much yeast and Go Ferm for a 1 gallon batch. I've purchased a more sensitive scale to measure out smaller amounts of yeast and nutrients. I'll use the scale to measure what I believe the correct amount of nutients are and test the result.

Ingredients

  • 112 oz Poland Spring Water
  • 2 pounds Neshanic Valley Blueberry Blossom Honey
    • 1.5 pounds for primary (1lb 8.1oz)
    • 0.5 pounds for back-sweetening (8.1oz)
  • 1 grams Lalvin D47 Yeast
  • 1/4 teaspoon North Mountain Powdered Wine Tanin
