Climax Brewing Co.

Climax Brewing Company is New Jersey’s oldest microbrewery with distribution in New Jersey, Pennsylvania and New York. Climax consistently garners praise in its industry and their web site attracts an average of 500 unique visitors per month. We launched a redesigned Climax Brewing Company web site in August 2006 built in the open source CMS, CMS Made Simple. The revised design is reflective of the company’s art and personality and provides expanded information about the company, their beers and where to find them.

The dude

I’m a designer and front-end developer living and working in New Jersey. Not the part you keep seeing on reality TV. The good part.

I believe in eye-catching design and in writing code that is semantic, standards compliant, SEO friendly and cross-browser compliant.

I deliver content managed web sites that let my clients get their content to the web with only basic computer know-how.

What that means in English is, I design good-looking web sites that work for your visitors and are easy for you to update and maintain.

I’ve recently fallen in love with Wordpress and am diligently working on my first premium theme. I also have a project in the works that’s so secret I call it the "Super-Secret Project." I’ll release more about that when I’m ready with the alpha.
